Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar.
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leaving agents (usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C.
  2. Grease a 20 cm round cake pan.
  3. In amedium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar untill fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in hte preheated oven, or untill the top of the cake springs back when lighly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over awire rack
